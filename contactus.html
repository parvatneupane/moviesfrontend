Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about MovieTalks

How do I create an account on MovieTalks?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, enter your email address, create a password, and you're all set. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster access.

Is MovieTalks free to use?

Yes, MovieTalks is completely free to use for browsing movie information, reading reviews, and creating watchlists. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will always remain free.

How can I submit a movie review?

After creating an account, navigate to any movie page and click on the "Write a Review" button. You can rate the movie on a scale of 1-5 stars and share your thoughts in the text box provided. All reviews are subject to our content guidelines.

How often is the movie database updated?

Our movie database is updated daily with new releases, upcoming movies, and the latest information. We source data from multiple providers to ensure accuracy and comprehensiveness.

Can I request a movie to be added to the database?

Absolutely! We welcome movie suggestions from our community. Use the contact form on this page to suggest movies you'd like to see added to our database. Please include as much information as possible about the movie.
